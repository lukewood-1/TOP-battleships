Not the usual Battlefield game

During development, I'd make this just like other existing battlefield games on the internet. But in the end, I chose not to, and that produced some changes. For example:

  • - In this version, you CAN place ships adjacent to other ships. I had implemented this but chose to rollback this one out;
  • - In this version, you will NOT see adjacent visual feedback when you attack a cell. The feedback will exist only in the target cell.
  • - If you don't have visual feedback on cells other than your targeted cells, you won't always know what ships you have left!

This makes this version harder and longer to finish than others as you don't know which direction you must hit to destroy the rest of a ship after the first hit. Have fun!
